# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025

## Introduction

JayDL ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## Information We Collect

### User Authentication
- **Google Account Information:** When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect your Google profile name and profile picture for display purposes.
- **User ID:** A unique identifier associated with your Google account to personalize your experience.

### Usage Data
- **Download History:** Records of videos/audio you download through our service (stored locally in your browser/account).
- **Preferences:** Your app settings and preferences.

### Technical Information
- **Browser Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system (for analytics and security purposes).
- **Cookies:** We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Your Google account password
- ❌ Your email address (unless you voluntarily provide it)
- ❌ Your contacts or personal data from Google
- ❌ Financial or payment information
- ❌ Sensitive personal information

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Authentication & Security:** To verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access
2. **Service Personalization:** To customize your experience and show your download history
3. **Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
4. **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your Google account secure.

## Third-Party Services

Our application uses:
- **Google OAuth:** For secure authentication (Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **YouTube API:** For accessing public video data (YouTube's Terms of Service apply)
- **Render:** For hosting our service (Render's Privacy Policy applies)

## Data Retention

- **Authentication Data:** Retained while you maintain an account
- **Download History:** Retained until you clear your browser data or account
- **Session Data:** Automatically deleted when you log out

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of our service
- Review and correct any inaccurate information

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
